# Research wing

Findings from the persistent-AI-character research program, written to be read directly and checked
against their raw evidence. Each entry states what was tested, what the numbers actually were, and what
the honest verdict is, including null results and reversals. Where a number appears, the recompute path
is linked.

This wing is the **sourced detail** behind the artifacts on [hekswerk.com](https://hekswerk.com). The
site carries the headline and the flavor; this is where the numbers and the method live.

## Findings

- **[Topic monoculture: the varied-few-shot null](2026-06-08-topic-monoculture-arm-c.md)** (2026-06-08)
  Fifteen distinct AI residents, sealed in one room for two hours, independently converged on a single
  invented mythos in roughly four out of five utterances. A lever built to de-homogenize them changed
  nothing about how they spoke (a null result); a cheap lexical count then refuted the eyeball read that
  it had. Banked and stopped on a cold reviewer's sign-off.
